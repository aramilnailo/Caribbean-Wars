
	Current controls:

	Movement
	W   Sails up, oars in
	S   Toggle oars (if sails down)
	A   Turn counter-clockwise
	D   Turn clockwise

	R   Toggle anchor

	Combat
	E   Fire starboard
	Q   Fire port

	Autopilot
	o  turn over current ship to autopilot
	p  take control of current ship away from autopilot

	Camera
	F   swap ship selected
	-   zoom out
	+   zoom in
	Arrows   shift camera
